Hala - Learn Emirati Arabic — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Introduction

Hala - Learn Emirati Arabic ("we", "our", "the app") is a language learning app developed and published by OVC Tech. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we access, why, and how it's handled.

Data We Access

  • Google Gemini AI — We use Google's Generative AI API to generate quiz questions for the AI Quiz and Boss Battle features. Only generic lesson content (phrases and vocabulary) is sent to the API to generate questions. No personal data is transmitted.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect your personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do NOT track your location
  • We do NOT sell or share any user data with third parties
  • We do NOT use any analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do NOT require account creation or login

Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device using AsyncStorage:

  • Learning progress (completed lessons, missions, XP)
  • Streak data and daily activity
  • Daily and weekly challenge progress
  • Flashcard review history
  • Listening time statistics
  • Settings and preferences (daily goal, Arabic text size, notifications)
  • Coins and achievement data

This data never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — We display ads powered by Google AdMob. Google may collect data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.
  • Google Generative AI (Gemini) — Used to generate quiz questions. Only lesson content is sent; no personal data is transmitted. See Google AI Terms.
  • Expo — The app framework used for push notifications. See Expo's Privacy Policy.

Permissions

  • Notifications — Optional. Used for daily practice reminders and challenge alerts.
  • Vibration — Used for haptic feedback during quizzes and games.
  • Internet — Required for AI-generated quizzes and displaying ads.

Data Retention & Deletion

All user data is stored locally on your device only. We do not store any of your data on external servers, so there is no server-side data to retain or delete.

How long we retain data: Local data (learning progress, streaks, preferences) is kept on your device for as long as the app is installed. There is no expiration — data persists until you choose to delete it.

How to delete your data:

  • Go to your device's Settings → Apps → Hala → Storage → Clear Data to erase all locally stored app data.
  • You can also use the "Reset All Progress" option in the app's Settings screen.
  • Uninstalling the app will also permanently delete all associated data from your device.

Since we do not collect or store any data on our servers, no further action is needed to delete your information. If you have any concerns, contact us at segmentbi@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages and does not require any personal information to use.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the "Last updated" date at the top.
