🇦🇪 Hala: Learn Emirati Arabic — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Introduction

Hala - Learn Emirati Arabic ("we", "our", "the app") is a language learning app developed and published by OVC Tech. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we access, why, and how it's handled.

Data We Access

  • AI Chat Messages — When you use the optional AI Tutor or AI Quiz features, your messages are sent to Google Gemini API for processing. These messages are not stored by us. Google's data handling is governed by their Generative AI Terms.
  • Text-to-Speech — The app uses your device's built-in speech engine to pronounce Arabic phrases. This is processed entirely on your device.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect your personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do NOT track your location
  • We do NOT sell or share any user data with third parties
  • We do NOT use any analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do NOT require account creation or login

Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device using AsyncStorage:

  • Learning progress and completed missions
  • Streak data and daily activity log
  • XP (experience points) and level
  • Daily challenge completion status
  • Flashcard confidence ratings
  • Daily goal and notification preferences
  • Coins balance

This data never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — We display advertisements powered by Google AdMob. Google may collect data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.
  • Google Gemini API — Optional AI features (Tutor, Quiz) use Google's Gemini API. Only your chat messages are sent; no personal data is transmitted. See Google AI Terms.
  • Expo — The app is built with Expo framework. Expo may handle push notification delivery if notifications are enabled.

Permissions

  • Notifications — Optional. Used for daily learning reminders and word-of-the-day notifications.
  • Internet — Required for AI features and advertisements. Core learning features work offline.

Data Retention & Deletion

All user data is stored locally on your device only. We do not store any of your data on external servers, so there is no server-side data to retain or delete.

How long we retain data: Local data (learning progress, settings, preferences) is kept on your device for as long as the app is installed. There is no expiration — data persists until you choose to delete it.

How to delete your data:

  • Go to your device's Settings > Apps > Hala > Storage > Clear Data to erase all locally stored app data.
  • Uninstalling the app will also permanently delete all associated data from your device.

Since we do not collect or store any data on our servers, no further action is needed to delete your information. If you have any concerns, contact us at support@ovctech.com.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages and collects no personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the "Last updated" date at the top.
