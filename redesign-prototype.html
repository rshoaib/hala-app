Onboarding (v2.0) — 60 seconds, 4 steps
1. What will you say first?
Travel · Make friends · Work · Just curious
2. Minutes per day?
3 (8 cards) · 5 (12 cards) · 10 (20 cards)
3. When will you practice?
Morning · Lunch · Evening implementation intention
4. Show the first card immediately.
No tour, no tutorial — the card teaches the interaction.

What changed

Cut (deleted, not hidden)
  • ai-tutor · ai-quiz · arcade · boss-battle
  • challenge · mission path · vocabulary screen
  • alphabet tab · radio tab · progress tab · practice tab · play tab · learn tab
  • leaderboards / leagues (would have added later)
Kept & reshaped
  • Flashcard engine → unified SRS session player
  • Alphabet + vocab + phrases → one card pool, interleaved
  • Streak + weekly dots + freezes → the spine of the "stack"
  • Radio → one "listen" card inside the daily session
  • Onboarding → compressed to 60s, ends with implementation intention

Core loop: Ebbinghaus spaced repetition · testing effect · streak loss-aversion. Every other mechanic has to earn its way back in v2.1.
